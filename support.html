Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my location?
You can allow the app to detect your location automatically via GPS, or select your city manually from the available cities list. Go to Settings > Location.
How do I enable prayer notifications?
Go to Settings > Notifications, then enable "Prayer Notifications". You can choose which prayers to be notified for and enable/disable sound and vibration.
How do I adjust Adhan or Iqama time?
Go to Settings and you'll find "Adhan Time Adjustment" and "Iqama Time Settings" options. You can adjust the time for each prayer independently.
How do I adjust the Hijri date?
Go to Settings > Hijri Date Adjustment. You can advance or delay the Hijri date by one day based on moon sighting in your region.
How do I view prayer times for other days?
On the main screen, swipe left or right to navigate between days (yesterday, today, tomorrow). The app automatically returns to today's page after 5 seconds.
What calculation method is used?
The app uses the Umm Al-Qura (Makkah) calculation method for prayer times, with Shafi'i/Hanbali/Maliki juristic method for Asr time.
How do I change the app language?
Go to Settings and choose your preferred language (Arabic or English). The app supports both RTL and LTR directions.
How do I change the theme from light to dark?
Go to Settings > Theme, and choose between Light, Dark, or System default.
Why are notifications not working?
Make sure: 1) Notifications are enabled in app settings, 2) The app is allowed to send notifications in device settings, 3) You've selected a city manually (notifications require manual mode).
Does the app need internet?
No, the app calculates prayer times locally on your device and doesn't need internet. Internet is only used for GPS location detection (optional).
